About Triangle Land

Triangle Land is a Jekyll-powered, open-source HTML5 website hosted on GitHub Pages. I have written everything by hand, with much of the design by rioforcea. The intent of this site is to serve as a live playground for items I cannot host on WordPress.com and to help me better learn client-side web development.

As of 31 March, 2014, Triangle Land is under heavy construction, and things will change, break, and move around without notice. I am sorry for any inconvenience this may cause.
