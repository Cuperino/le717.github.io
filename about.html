About Me

Hello! I am Triangle717 (pronounced lee seven one seven), sometimes known as le717 (pronounced lee seven seventeen).

I am a programmer. After discovering the world of programming in October 2013 by going through the Codcademy HTML Fundamentals track and following A Byte of Python by
Swaroop C H, I discovered what God had prepared me for though the hardships I have endured.

I am currently attending technical college to obtain my associates degree in website design and development. Projected graduation date is Spring or Summer 2015.
