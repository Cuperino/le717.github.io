About Me

Hello! I am Triangle717 (pronounced lee seven one seven), sometimes known as le717 (pronounced lee seven seventeen). I am years old, harkening from Triangle Land, Georgia, USA.
I am a Christian, born and raised in a Christian home, and I am not afraid to publicly proclaim my faith in God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ), and God the Holy Spirit.

I am a programmer of nearly one and a half years. After discovering the world of programming in October 2013 by going through the Codcademy HTML Fundamentals track and following A Byte of Python by
Swaroop C H, I discovered what God had prepared me for though the hardships I have endured.

I am currently attending technical college to obtain my associates degree in website design and development. Projected graduation date is Spring or Summer 2015.

I am a strong supporter of open-source programs. You will find all my programming work on GitHub. Links to various projects and downloads for them can be found on the Source Code and Downloads pages of my blog, respectively.
